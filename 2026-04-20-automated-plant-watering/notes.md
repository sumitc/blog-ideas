# Notes: Automating Plant Watering — Still Ever"green"

## Origin Story
- Original LinkedIn post (2020): "Nothing gives more satisfaction than monitoring & watering your 50+ plants from 600+ Kms away!!"
- Post went viral with comments asking for a "how-to" blog
- Friends (Naresh, Amit, Biplav) all wanted the setup guide

## Key Narrative Points
- Problem: 50+ plants at home, frequently traveling for work (600+ km away)
- Neighbors/friends not a reliable solution long-term
- Built a DIY smart watering system using off-the-shelf parts
- Can monitor soil moisture and trigger watering remotely from phone
- System has been running for 5+ years — low maintenance, reliable
