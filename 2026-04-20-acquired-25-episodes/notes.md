# Notes: Acquired — 25 Episodes in Less Than 2 Months!

## Origin Story
- Late February 2026: Sumit visiting Microsoft Redmond campus
- Colleagues recommended Acquired.fm — a Seattle-based podcast about tech acquisitions
- Started from Episode 1
- By April 20, 2026 — finished 25 episodes (~2 months)
- Hooked from the start

## About Acquired.fm
- Hosts: Ben Gilbert and David Rosenthal
- Format: Deep-dive episodes on major tech acquisitions and company stories
- Based in Seattle
- Known for incredibly well-researched, long-form episodes (often 2-4 hours)
- One of the most popular tech/business podcasts

## Episodes That Stuck
1. **Disney Acquisitions — Pixar, Lucasfilm**
   - Connected with Bob Iger's book "The Ride of a Lifetime" which Sumit has read
   - The courage to acquire creative companies and let them stay independent
   - Iger's leadership philosophy

2. **NeXT**
   - Steve Jobs' wilderness years
   - All the moments Jobs inspired the world around him
   - The comeback story that led back to Apple

3. **YouTube, Android, Instagram**
   - The humbling origins of these giants
   - How small, scrappy teams built what billions use today
   - The acquisitions that shaped the modern internet
   - We take these for granted, but each had a fascinating founding story

## Key Themes
- Great stories behind companies we take for granted
- Leadership, vision, and timing
- The humbling beginnings of tech giants
- Learning from history to build the future
- The joy of being a perpetual learner
