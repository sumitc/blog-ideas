# Notes: AI Tools — The New Equalizer

## Origin Story
- Sumit was a jury member at ProdX 2026, IIT Hyderabad's flagship product case competition
- Part of Horizon '26 fest, organized by Finance and Consulting Club, IIT Hyderabad
- In collaboration with The Product Folks
- 4000+ registrations, multiple online rounds, grand finale on campus
- Fellow judges: Ruturaj Dhekane (Principal PM, Microsoft/Bing), Mohan Kosuri (AI Product Leader, ServiceNow)

## The Insight
- All top 7 finalist teams had remarkably similar:
  - Competitive analysis
  - Strategy diagnosis
  - Suggested improvements
- Why? They were all using AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, etc.) — getting similar outputs
- The AI tools "equalized" the baseline — everyone had access to the same intelligence
- What differentiated the WINNER: real human creativity layered on top
  - Original thinking
  - Unique angles the AI didn't suggest
  - Storytelling ability
  - Bold, non-obvious bets

## Key Thesis
- AI tools are the great equalizer — they raise the floor for everyone
- But they don't raise the ceiling — that's still human creativity
- In a world where everyone has the same AI, differentiation comes from:
  1. The questions you ask (not the answers you get)
  2. The creative leaps you make
  3. Your lived experience and unique perspective
  4. The courage to go against the obvious
